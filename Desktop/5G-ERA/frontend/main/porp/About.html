About us

The Entelechy company is a world-leading wireless technology innovation company under Qualcomm. We are committed to inventing breakthrough basic technologies. It has revolutionized the way the world connects, computes and communicates. Qualcomm's invention connected mobile phones to the Internet and opened the era of mobile Internet. Today, Qualcomm's foundational technology has empowered the entire mobile ecosystem.

Qualcomm brings the advantages of technology to emerging industries such as automobiles and the Internet of Things. and computers, creating a new world where people and everything can communicate and interact smoothly.

The basic technology we invented has been widely applied to the entire solar sector through the technology licensing model, and has become a strong backing for customers and partners to innovate, compete and develop on a global scale. This model began in 1995 and is the foundation of Qualcomm's core mission, values and culture.

Our purpose of launching this platform is very simple, that is to enable those who believe in us and support us to live a better life and embark on the road to wealth together. Entelechy can help corporate factories, logistics networks, energy companies and other institutions operate more efficiently, consciously and with purpose.
